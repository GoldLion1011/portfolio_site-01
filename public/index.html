Intro

A nearly-native of Tulsa, OK. By the way, check out my awesome work.

As a junior Fullstack Web Developer, I have uncovered a passion for frontend development and UX/UI design. I love experimenting with and crafting intuitive, visually appealing user experiences that bring websites to life. With a strong foundation in web technologies and a keen eye for design, I enjoy combining my technical skills with creative problem-solving to build dynamic and user-friendly applications. I am constantly exploring new frontend frameworks, staying up to date with the latest industry trends, and honing my skills to deliver high-quality web solutions. I have a background in healthcare, wellness, and the visual arts and have worked on various projects that involve building responsive and accessible websites. I believe in the power of collaboration and strive to create seamless experiences that engage and delight users.

Work

  • DraCo

    We were challenged to develop a frontend website using the prompt "Draco dormiens nunquam titillandus" for a secretive client. We leaned into the theme and decided to go with a high-end dragon luxury boutique with a Firebase backend. Our tech stack implemented HTML, Javascript, Firebase, Firestore, Canva and Bootstrap.

  • Smiling School

    I was asked to recreate a website advertising a school for "smiling skills" that utilized implementations of vanilla JS, Bootstrap, Figma, HTML, and CSS. My codebase for this project was my own previous work, so I had to make sure to cross all the t's! I am pretty happy with how this turned out, and I loved feeling the satisfaction of finishing a full, multi-page site on my own.

  • Headphones

    This was my first full frontend project for my first trimester as a Fullstack web dev student at Holberton School, Tulsa. The tech stack used was Figma, HTML and CSS.
